About UA Dict

UA Dict is Uttaranchal dictionary contains words normally used in the hilly reigon of the Northern India especially in Garhwal and Kumaon Reigon.

The purpose of this website to acquaint the generations (originally belongs to the uttarakhand) with their native dilects. As dialicts are diminishing day by day basis. And soon we don't find enough people who speaks our dialects.

This webpage contains the dialets word(Garwali and Kumaoni) and their meaning in English and Hindi.
